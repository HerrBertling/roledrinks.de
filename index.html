A11y Meet-up

What? A bunch of people coming together to talk about accessibility!
When? 18th of June somewhere in the afternoon.
Till? Michiel's dinosaur onsie begins to smell.
Where? ImpactHub, Haarlemmerweg 10C, 1014 BE Amsterdam.

Is this going to cost me anything? We will cover the venue, but you'll have to buy your own drinks.

Sounds good right? If you want to come, tell Michiel on Twitter or send Michiel an e-mail. We are also looking for a few people to give a so called lightning talk—these are short talks that last anywhere between 5 and 10 minutes; so if you're up for that, please tell us.
